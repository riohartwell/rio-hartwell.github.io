About

I'm Rio Hartwell, a freelance editor specializing in academic book manuscripts for scholars in the humanities and social sciences.

I work with authors at every stage of the publishing process—from shaping a book proposal to delivering a press-ready manuscript. My clients include faculty and researchers whose work has appeared with Oxford University Press, Cambridge University Press, University of Chicago Press, Columbia University Press, University of Massachusetts Press, and other leading academic imprints.

What sets my approach apart is continuity: rather than handing your manuscript off to different specialists at each phase, you work with one editor who understands your argument, your voice, and your goals from start to finish.

My background combines graduate training—I did my doctoral work at Arizona State University—with professional certification in Editing and Publishing from the University of Chicago. I've worked on manuscripts spanning Latin American politics, urban studies, criminology, social movements, and American literature.
